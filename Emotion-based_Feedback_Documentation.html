  Introduction

  Purpose

  Our collective acknowledgment of the importance of having a tool that gives real and accurate feedback led us in the decision for the creation of this application. The main goal is to bring closer together the people who need feedback and the people willing to provide it. It also reduces the time spend reading all the reviews, by interpreting the selected emotions beforehand, easing the provider's process of creating a bigger picture of the customer's emotions and opinions regarding the displayed product or service.

  Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions

  The intended audience of this paper are developers, testers, project managers, marketing staff, users and any other category that is interested in developing or using our application. In case of inexperienced regular users who want to know more about the ways of using, we recommend our Overall Description, User Interfaces, System Features, as well as any other section that raises interest, starting with the Introduction, Overall Description, External Interface Requirements and finishing off with Other Nonfunctional Requirements.
 We will address any category that can be uploaded and reviewed, being a service, person, event, object etc. as a product.

  Product Scope

  Our initiative is to bring together any type of provider to their clients. By doing so, the time spent by the providers of goods is be exponentially reduced and they have have a clearer picture of their customers' opinions on their service. It is useful especially for people that want to launch a new product and that do not have the resources to analyse the market on their own before taking the big step. Our goal is also to create a safe space accessible to anyone that wants to share their experience and emotions on a product, or service.

  Overall Description

  Product Perspective

  Our product checks both the functional and quality requirements for products of its kind, so we will go deeper into some of its most important characteristics, which will be detailed in the sections below.
  The functional requirements that define its behaviour are the possibility of creating an account if not existent, leaving room for users that are not logged to use the application in a similar manner to reviewers. However, the functionality that describes uploading products as forms that expire, as well as the interpreted results is only available to the users that already have an account.
 The application also fits quality requirements, as it is precise in generating information, highly throughput and exceedingly safe for both registered users and guests, in terms of confidentiality and data management.
  The problem which we identified is the well known need of business owners and management teams, as well as artists and regular people to centralise and analyse information about their specific product or provided service. These features shift the product perspective as a powerful tool that can significantly reduce, or even fully dismiss the time spent researching. As users leave anonymous reviews, the level of honesty is increased and the application’s analysis of the reviews are be indubitably accurate.
The evaluated emotions are selected from the Plutchik’s Wheel of Emotions, shown below. Each "petal" represents an emotion, while the closer to the center the emotion is, the more intense the emotion is felt.

  Product Functions

We will split the functionalities into more subcategories, as follows:

 Logged Users Functions

Creating an account.
 2. Uploading a form as a “post” and request reviews from any category of people that are willing to help, or are interested in giving their review, in case they personally benefited from its usage, or from the provided service, such as events, firms, sole proprietorships and any other kind of service providers.
 3. Set an expiration date for the uploaded form and wait for the reviews to pile up. Once the expiration date has passed, a graphical analysis of the messages is provided by the application, that consists of elegantly structured data, that sums up the emotions that people felt while writing the message. The form uploaders are also able to see the emotions inserted by the reviewers for each aspect they chose.
4. See personalised suggestions on products they might also want to review.
 5. Access their personal data and update any information about themselves.

 Unlogged Users Functions

1. Anyone can access the site and leave a review, and only their message are stored, which makes the process fully anonymous. For users that have an account, however, the message is linked to their information.The information is not publicly accessible, as it is only used by the application in order to better detect the patterns of behaviour each client has and provide a better analysis of the feedback for the product uploaders.
 2. See what products are tranding and acces reviews uploaded by other users.

  User Classes and Characteristics

  The indentified users from the practical point of view are people that present the difficulties presented above.
 Approaching from a more theoretical perspective, the identified users are split into three main categories, as users without accounts, with limited access to the provided features, and users with accounts that have different accessibility levels, depending on whether they uploaded a product or service, or just reviewed other posts anonymously.
 For a clearer view, the Use Case Diagram can be consulted, found in the Software Interfaces section, containing the types of users and functionalities.

  Operating Environment

Given the fact that this is an web application, it is browser-dependent, so the only requirement is for a browser to be present, in order for the application to work properly.

  System Features

Use case diagram:

The full project structure:

  Creating an account

Priority: High.
Stimulus: Once the user is on the Homepage, if they want to add a product, having an account is mandatory. By clicking on the "Sign In" button and providing an username, email, age and password, the information is sent and inserted in the database. It will be accessed everytime the user wants to log in, or new statistics are requested.
Functional Requirements: In case the process has an error, the user can refresh the page safely and try inserting the information again. The invalid inputs will also create an error, such as wrongly formatted emails, in whitch case the process will hold, and the user has to type a correct format.

 Adding a new product

Priority: High.
Stimulus: After possesing an account, the user will be able to create add a new product by accessing the top button. A new form is dispalyed and the uploader can choose the category and basic points to be reviewed, and add new features that are specific to their product.Adding a deadline is mandatory. A picture and a basic desription can also be provided. The information about the product is then added in the database.
Functional Requirements: If an error occurs, the inserted information about the project will be lost and must be inserted again.

  Reviewing product

Priority: High.
Stimulus: By accessing the full list of products from a category, or by scrolling through the popular, or "For You" sections and pressing the button displayed for adding a review, a new page with a form will open. The form will be generated based on the infoarmation in the database, as the uploader chose which aspects to be reviewed.
Functional Requirements:In case of interruptions, the form will not be saved.

  Manage products

Priority: Medium.
Stimulus: The uploaders can access the list of products on their account. The ongoing forms and the ones that have reached their deadline have different functionalities. The on going ones can be edited or deleted, while the finalised ones can only provide the files containig the statistics, extracted from the database and intrepreted and formatted in the backend.
Functional Requirements:If the deletion of a product is done by mistake, the product will still be available for a few more days on their page.

  Update account information

Priority: Low.
Stimulus: By accessing the editing page, users can update, as the information will then be sent and updated in the database.
Functional Requirements: In case of error, the unmodified information, before editing, is being kept.

  Search for a desired product

Priority: Low.
Stimulus:By accessing the side menu, the products will be displayed and the user will be able to choose what product they want to review.
Functional Requirements:The full list of products must be displayed, by most recent, or by the most reviewed.
