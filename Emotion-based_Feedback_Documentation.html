  Introduction

  Purpose

  Our collective acknowledgment of the importance of having a tool that gives real and accurate feedback led us in the decision for the creation of this application. The main goal is to bring closer together the people who need feedback, and the people willing to provide it. It also reduces the time spend reading all the reviews, by interpreting the messages beforehand, easing the provider's process of having a bigger picture of the customer's emotions and opinions regarding the displayed product or service.

  Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions

  The intended audience of this paper are developers, testers, project managers, marketing staff, users and any other category that is interested in developing or using our application. In case of inexperienced regular users who want to know more about the ways of using, we recommend our Overall Description, User Interfaces, System Features, as well as any other section that raises interest, starting with the Introduction, Overall Description, External Interface Requirements and finishing off with Other Nonfunctional Requirements.

  Product Scope

  Our initiative is to bring together any type of provider to their clients. By doing so, the time spent by the providers of goods will be exponentially reduced and they will have a clearer picture of their customers' opinions on their service. It is useful especially for people that want to launch a new product and that do not have the resources to analyse the market on their own before taking the big step. Our goal is also to create a safe space accessible to anyone that wants to share their experience or thoughts on a product, or service.
