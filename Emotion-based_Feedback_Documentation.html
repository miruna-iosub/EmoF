  Introduction

  Purpose

  Our collective acknowledgment of the importance of having a tool that gives real and accurate feedback led us in the decision for the creation of this application. The main goal is to bring closer together the people who need feedback, and the people willing to provide it. It also reduces the time spend reading all the reviews, by interpreting the messages beforehand, easing the provider's process of having a bigger picture of the customer's emotions and opinions regarding the displayed product or service.

  Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions

  The intended audience of this paper are developers, testers, project managers, marketing staff, users and any other category that is interested in developing or using our application. In case of inexperienced regular users who want to know more about the ways of using, we recommend our Overall Description, User Interfaces, System Features, as well as any other section that raises interest, starting with the Introduction, Overall Description, External Interface Requirements and finishing off with Other Nonfunctional Requirements.

  Product Scope

  Our initiative is to bring together any type of provider to their clients. By doing so, the time spent by the providers of goods is be exponentially reduced and they have have a clearer picture of their customers' opinions on their service. It is useful especially for people that want to launch a new product and that do not have the resources to analyse the market on their own before taking the big step. Our goal is also to create a safe space accessible to anyone that wants to share their experience or thoughts on a product, or service.

  Overall Description

  Product Perspective

  Our product checks both the functional and quality requirements for products of its kind, so we will go deeper into some of its most important characteristics, which will be detailed in the sections below.
  The functional requirements that define its behaviour are the possibility of creating an account if not existent, leaving room for users that are not logged to use the application in a similar manner to reviewers. However, the functionality that describes uploading products as forms that expire, as well as the interpreted results is only available to the users that already have an account.
The application also fits quality requirements, as it is precise in generating information, highly throughput and exceedingly safe for both registered users and guests, in terms of confidentiality while using the app and data management.
  The problem which we identified is the well known need of business owners and management teams, as well as artists and regular people to centralise and analyse information about their specific product or provided service. These features shift the product perspective as a powerful tool that can significantly reduce, or even fully cover the time spent researching. As users leave anonymous reviews, the level of honesty is increased and the application’s analysis of the reviews are be indubitably accurate.

  Product Functions

We will split the functionalities into more main categories, as follows:

 Logged Users Functions

Creating an account.
 2. Uploading a form as a “post” and request reviews from any category of people that are willing to help, or are interested in giving their review, in case they personally benefited from its usage, or from the provided service, such as events, firms, sole proprietorships and any other kind of service providers.
 3. Set an expiration date for the uploaded form and wait for the reviews to pile up. Once the expiration date has passed, a graphical analysis of the messages is provided by the application, that consists of elegantly structured data, that sums up the emotions that people felt while writing the message. The form uploaders are also able to see the detected emotion of each comment.
4. See personalised suggestions on products they might also want to review.
 5. Access their personal data and update any information about themselves.

 Unlogged Users Functions

Anyone can access the site and leave a review, and only their message are stored, which makes the process fully anonymous. For users that have an account, however, the message is linked to their information.The information is not publicly accessible, as it is only used by the application in order to better detect the patterns of behaviour each client has and provide a better analysis of the feedback for the product uploaders.
 2. See what products are tranding and acces reviews uploaded by other users.

  User Classes and Characteristics

  The indentified users from the practical point of view are people that present the difficulties presented above.
 Approaching from a more theoretical perspective, the identified users are split into three main categories, as users without accounts, with limited access to the provided features, and users with accounts that have different accessibility levels, depending on whether they uploaded a product or service, or just reviewed other posts anonymously.
